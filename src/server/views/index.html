ADMINISTRATOR

The police administrator is logged into the system and presented with administrative options where he/she can have access to the crime database, view custom analysis of the data and use the predictive policing technology to plan force deployment for effective vigilance.

Login





CRIME MAP

The user can specify the location of his/her interest and a map marking the criminal activities in that territory is produced. The map can be filtered on the basis of date, type and time of the crime in that vicinity. A heat map may also be obtained by clicking on the ‘View Heat Map’ option. The user can click on the ‘Analysis’ tab to view the graphical analysis of the crimes.
