FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)

We have a list of frequently asked questions............ So go ahead, feel free to ask questions and join our movement in making public spaces safe for all especially women, girls and children and spreading awareness about safety.


Q: How do I report if I’m being abused and I’m shy to lodge an FIR?
Ans: Women who are subjected to repeated sexual violence and sexual harassment may shy away from standing up against the perpetrator. You can add your crime details on our app. It is available on our play store. The app lets you add the crimes and further maps it on the crime map anonymously. A simple way to lodge in your complaint without revealing your identity.

Q: I’m a solo traveller. I want to know which places are safe for me to travel?
Ans: The analytics section of satark web application provides you with the places percentage of safety for women. You just need to enter the location you want to travel to and you are done.

Q: My son is in 10th standard. His coaching is quite far away from home. How do I know if the place is crime safe?
Ans: You can check the safety of a particular place by feeding in the details of the place and see the pie chart of different crimes in that place. This will ensure the safety of your child beforehand.

Q: I’m being teased by someone and am reluctant to share it with people I know. What do I do?
Ans: Sometimes women don’t know what, how and whom to share with people they know. In such cases, sharing your story in the Share your Story section of our app will let you keep in touch with the people who have suffered the same nd who are willing to help. This is a great platform for people to share their stories.

Q: In case of an emergency, who do I contact?
Ans: Go to the emergency section in the web application to find helpline numbers. Our Satark Nagrik Mobile app also provides with an SOS button in case of an emergency.
